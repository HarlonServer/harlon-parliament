City of Harlon General Property Limits Act

HB 3-092

Author: muffinbarde

15 Dec 2023

Opening Declaration

  1. In the City of Harlon currently there is no limit on how many properties you can own. This can lead to abandoned districts (for example Faybury at the moment).
  2. This Act aims to solve this issue by introducing such a property limit using already existing (but unused) plugin features.

Information

  1. After passage of this Act, the properties sold to players in CoH shall progressively get limited by area to the following limits, which are based on the factors of property availability and size of the area:

  1. These regulations will only apply to properties sold by HarlonGov.
  2. These regulations shall not apply to
  1. already bought property.
  2. rentably properties.
  3. free properties.
  4. properties in not above mentioned areas unless staff decides so.

Requirements

  1. This bill will require staff to set up the property limits using the already existing ARM plugin. More information in Other Information.

Implementation and timeline

  1. The bill shall get implemented by staff in December 2023 and January 2024, meaning the full implementation shall be finished on January 31, 2024.

Other Information

  1. I, muffinbarde, as the author of this Act, will take all responsibilities for implementing the Act using the plugin.
