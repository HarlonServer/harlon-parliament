Government Publications Act

HB 3-090

Author: Westray

13 Dec 2023

Opening Declaration

  1. The purpose of this bill is to establish a standard for transparency reports from the government of Harlon. It will require the government of Harlon to post regular transparency reports on government events every two months.

Information

  1. The government shall, at least once every two months, publish a report on happenings with regards to updates about the state of Harlon, cabinet changes, and actions of the Prime Minister. The government is welcome to publish this report more frequently, however that is at their discretion.
  2. The Prime Minister will be responsible for delivering the report. This responsibility may be delegated to other members of government, however the Prime Minister will be the one held accountable if a report is not published regularly.
  3. The government will have the option to decide what formatting they wish to use, as long as it is in a publicly accessible document (such as a Google Doc, PDF, etc.)
  4. The report must include the following information:
  1. The current members of cabinet
  2. The number of people employed by the government
  3. The current government balance
  4. Notes taken during any public cabinet meetings if applicable
  5. Any other additional information may be included, but is not required.

Implementation and timeline

  1. This bill will require the government to publish a monthly transparency report at least once every two months.
  2. This bill shall take effect on January 1, 2024. That means the first time the government will be required to publish a report will be March 1, 2024.
