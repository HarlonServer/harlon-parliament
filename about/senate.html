The Senate is a two-step democracy system in the Harlon Legislation system, and as of right now, there are 6 members. The Senate has the responsibility of approving or denying legislature passed by the House of Assembly, as well as approving the Parliament Speaker. After voting, they have the right to agree or reject the legislature posted by the Parliament, even the bills that have gained full HoA support.

To become a member of the Senate, one must be a staff member with the Moderator rank or higher and not be serving as Parliamentary Staff. The Crown must also approve all new Senate members. There can be no Senate additions until a Parliament election occurs.
