The Speaker of the Parliament of the Federation of Harlon is the presiding officer of the House of Assembly, the lower house of the Parliament of Harlon. The Speaker makes order papers and leads the sessions. The Speaker is appointed by the Senate before the General Election. The current Speaker is Dirt0066.
