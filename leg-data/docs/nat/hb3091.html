Going Once, Going Twice, SOLD Act

HB 3-091

Author: Westray

14 Dec 2023

Opening Declaration

  1. The purpose of this bill is to implement an auction plugin within Harlon.

Information

  1. Market auctions will be established whereby people can auction off items of high value to others. Rather than direct selling, auctions will allow for people to bid on these items.
  2. While auctions can continue to be held informally on other discords, the main auctions in Harlon will be held in-game in order to encourage people to join the server to make bids.

Implementation and timeline

  1. This will require staff to implement an auction plugin with freedom to configure as reasonable, with the following possible free options:
  1. https://www.spigotmc.org/resources/auctionhouse.61836/
  2. https://www.spigotmc.org/resources/crazy-auctions.25219/
  3. An alternative one if staff find a better one. Must be for auctions.
  1. The deadline for implementation will be on January 15, 2024.
