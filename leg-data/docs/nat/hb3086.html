December 2023 - January 2024 Budget

HB 3-087

Author: yoshipie_

23 Nov 2023

  1. Budget Timeline
  • This budget is to last from:
  • Throughout December 2023 to January 2024
  • Unless stated otherwise in future laws, this budget is also to be used again for the February 2024 to March 2024 and the April 2024 to May 2024 periods.
  1. Budget Spending
  • Below is the following on what the budget will allocate:

Government Entity

Budget

Ministry of Home Affairs

  • 110,000 Harlonian Pounds
  • 10,000 Harlonian Pounds to be allocated towards hosting domestic summer events.
  • 35,000 Harlonian Pounds to be allocated towards compensating winners and players in the 2023 Spring Olympics
  • Any spending not outlined can be decided by the Minister of Home Affairs or Parliament in bills, amendments and acts.

Ministry of Infrastructure and Development

  • 20,000 Harlonian Pounds
  • Any spending not outlined can be decided by the Minister of Infrastructure and Development or Parliament in bills, amendments and acts.

Ministry of Foreign Affairs

  • 42,000 Harlonian Pounds
  • 10,000 Pounds are to be allocated for the establishment of embassies in other nations.
  • 6,000 Pounds are to be allocated to pay each Ambassador at least 500 Pounds once a month in accordance with Executive Order 501.
  • 20,000 Pounds are to be allocated for global intelligence programs, alliance-building, and a restructuring of ambassadors.
  • Any spending not outlined can be decided by the Minister of Foreign Affairs or Parliament in bills, amendments and acts.

Ministry of Finance

  • 90,000 Harlonian Pounds
  • 30,000 Pounds are to be allocated for Business Funding.
  • Any spending not outlined can be decided by the Minister of Finance or Parliament in bills, amendments and acts.

Armed Forces of Harlon

  • 35,000 Harlonian Pounds
  • 9,000 Pounds are to be allocated for the Royal Army of Harlon.
  • Any spending not outlined can be decided by the Chief of the Defence Staff or Parliament in bills, amendments and acts.

Ministry of Justice

  • 50,000 Harlonian Pounds
  • Any spending not outlined can be decided by the Minister of Justice or Parliament in bills, amendments and acts.

Prime Minister Affairs & Direct Administration

  • 7,000 Harlonian Pounds
  • Any spending not outlined can be decided by the Prime Minister or Parliament in bills, amendments and acts.

Miscellaneous Government Spending

  • 70,000 Harlonian Pounds
  • 30,000 Pounds are to be allocated for server promotion compensation in accordance with the Media and Server Promotions of 2023 Act.
  • Any spending not outlined can be decided by the Prime Minister or Parliament in bills, amendments and acts.

The total budget will comprise of 424,000 Harlonian Pounds.
